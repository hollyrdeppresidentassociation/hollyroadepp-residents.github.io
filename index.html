Building a stronger community, together

The Holly Road & East Park Parade Residents Association exists to represent the interests of local residents, improve our shared spaces, and foster a strong sense of community pride.

We work closely with the local council, organise neighbourhood events, and keep residents informed about issues that matter to them.
